Refles
Concept Establishment
Game Mechanics Design
Visual Identity Design
Figma Prototyping
A gamified self-reflection tool which facilitates practicing reflection as a daily routine. It offers a guided self-reflective learning experience by recording their thoughts, behaviours and associated character strengths, which ultimately improves their psychological wellness and enables self-growth. This capstone project is awarded Tutors’ Choice at PolyU Design Annual Show 2019.

Features
Guided Diary
Guided diary provides user a reflective thinking structure to follow and stimulate their thinking. By following every step, they are encouraged to think more and deeper into their situations, which ultimately makes it an effective learning process.
Origin Story
The in-game characters, also known as the Greatest, represent the character strengths of the user. By interacting with the Greatest, user can discover each of their personal stories and understand their thinking process. It is a great source of inspiration for reflecting their personal strengths beside their daily routine.
Expert's Advice
The Greatest can assist the user by studying their diary and giving the most suitable advice. These advice can be academic studies, theories, personal stories or history events. These feedbacks not only provide new direction for future reflection, but also the guidance they needed for developing their strengths.
Theory
Character Strengths and Virtues
Refles is based on the scientifically proven studies of the Character Strengths and Virtues (CSV) framework. The CSV framework provides a scientific classification and measure for the positive strengths or traits in human beings. It identifies 24 measurable character strengths and organizes them into 6 classes of virtue. Every individual possesses all of the character strengths but in different degrees. For more information, please visit the VIA Institute on Character.
Research
Problem & Opportunity
Three major issues are found for the common practice of self-reflection in our background research. Most of our target audience reflect on themselves, but no further action is taken since the process is not documented. Second, self-reflection without feedback lacks the perspective for an all-around thinking and learning experience. Third, The lack of indicators for measuring improvement makes self-reflection a frustrating learning process.
Workshop: Sharing
The goal of the first workshop is to understand users’ behaviour on practicing reflection and their motivation. Seven participants are invited to share daily routines and personal values through activies like journey mapping and card sorting. We discover that our participants wanted to better utilize their wasted time, especially while commuting. They also share that personal development is their most reviewed topic.
Workshop: Simulation
The second workshop aims to understand users’ quality of reflection and their opinion on feedback. We host a 3-day 36-hour self-reflection simulation via WhatsApp with four participants. They are invited to private groups with facilitators acting as their personal feedback provider. They are encouraged to share their thoughts anytime and rate the feedback. The chat records and their opinion from the post-simulation discussion are collected and organized as transcript for further analysis. Our participants are generally positive on our feedback and advice, and generally agreed that they reflect more deeply. We conclude that having emotional support is as important as having constructive advice.
Workshop: Playtesting
We invite seven participants and learn about their learning process and engagement while playtesting the Refles prototype. Participants play though the introduction and tutorial sessions of Refles and later fill in the questionnaire on their understanding of our prototype. The result is mostly positive. The majority of our participants are willing to try using Refles in their daily routine and develop their habit of self-reflecting.

Game Mechanics
Think Tank Assembly
Besides five of the starting characters, there are more Greatest to be discovered on Refles. Refles recommends user to discover and train their strengths with an appropriate assistant. Recruit them all to explore the full spectrum of 24 character strengths.
Evolution Unlocking
There are also Evolution for the Greatest. Most of the Greatest has several significant moments across their lives, such as adolescence, adulthood and the peak moment of their career. As user progresses on Refles, they can unlock their different stages and receive more rewards.
City Management
Every building in Refles serves a different purpose for the user. Museum saves all of the user's progress and City Hall connects users with each other. There are also six virtue-specific buildings for categorizing and analyzing different types of reflection.
Essence Collection & Upgrades
Essence acts as the in-game currency and the experience for levelling up the Greatest. It can be acquired by interacting with the Greatest, such as playing mini-games, unlocking new stories and receiving advice from them.

Features
Compilation of Minigames
Each level aims to provide a unique experience with an easy-to-learn mechanic and detailed tutorial. Players are able to learn more about some of the essential steps on beekeeping in a fun and engaging way.
Realistic Reference
All of the information in the game is collected through interviews with beekeepers in Tsuen Wan, Hong Kong. Our team visited their homes and documented their daily routines. The result serves as an important reference and inspiration when designing game mechanics.
Cross-platform Experience
With the power of HTML5, the game can be enjoyed across most of the mobile platform, including iOS and Android. It also avoids the trouble of installation and compatibility issues.
Nearbuy
Experience Design
Origami Studio Prototyping
Noodl Prototyping
A mobile app designed for families to organize their groceries and give reminders to every family member in an appropriate time. Any family member who locates near a targeted shop will receive notifications from the app, and optionally, messages or phone calls from their family members.

Features
Family Collaboration
One family shares one grocery list. Every member of the family has the abilities to help shopping for groceries and remind their family members when they are approaching shops.
Unique Reminders
Nearbuy notifies the ones who walk near targeted shops based on their GPS location. Other members are also notified and given the chance to message or call to give reminders.
Automatic Checks
The system monitors every item entered by the users on the grocery list and notifies the family when items needed to be refilled. If items have not been refilled in time, the items will be prioritized on the list and marked as urgent.

Overview
Protagonists
Ryu and Chun come from the Vipers gang in mainland. While Ryu is an aggressive and reckless thug, his partner Chun is the mastermind of the gang who smartly strategizes their plans. Tung is a university student who runs into debts and in need of cash. She is recruited by Ryu and Chun for the upcoming plans.
Story Synopsis
They need funding and they go to a underground gambling den for a quick burglary. Later when they are looking for their informant in a drug dealers’ warehouse, they are drawn into a gunfight between the gangs and the police. They track down a bank manager in a strip club and threaten him to help them with photos of him. Finally, they are prepared for the heist.
Core Mechanic
The core mechanic involves three major steps. Players explore the level and discover puzzles to solve and targets to locate. They face enemies along the way and need to fight them with different weapons and strategies. Once they meet all the objectives, they have to escape the level and progress to the next chapter.
