A web-based mobile game for a collaborative project on promoting beekeeping culture in Hong Kong. The game simulates different regular activities for beekeepers, such as building apiaries, searching for bee nests, killing wasps and extracting honey. For more details on our project, please visit our project site.

COLLECTION OF MINIGAMES
Each level aims to provide a unique experience with an easy-to-learn mechanic and detailed tutorial. Players are able to learn more about some of the major steps on beekeeping in a fun and casual way.
FIRST-HAND RESEARCH
All of the information in the game is collected through interviews with beekeepers in Tsuen Wan, Hong Kong. Our team visited their homes and documented their daily routines. The result serves as an important reference and inspiration when designing game mechanics.
CROSS-PLATFORM EXPERIENCE
With the power of HTML5, the game can be enjoyed across most of the mobile platform, including iOS and Android. It also avoids the trouble of installation and compatibility issues.

NOODL PROTOTYPING
A mobile app designed for families organizing their groceries and giving reminders to their grocery shoppers of the family in an appropriate time. Any family member who locates near a targeted shop will receive notifications from the app, and ideally messages or phone calls from their family members.

FAMILY COLLABORATION
One family shares one grocery list. Every member of the family has the abilities to help shopping for groceries and remind their family members when they are approaching shops.
UNIQUE REMINDERS
Nearbuy notifies the ones who walk near targeted shops based on their GPS location. Other members are also notified and given the chance to message or call to give reminders.
AUTOMATIC CHECKS
The system monitors every item entered by the users on the grocery list and notifies the family when items needed to be refilled. If items have not been refilled in time, the items will be prioritized on the list and marked as urgent.
